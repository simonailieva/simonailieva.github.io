Morocco

From the fine sandy beaches of the Atlantic, through the Sahara desert to the high regions of the Atlas Mountains, extends Morocco. We get to know Marrakech - the capital of three Berber and three Arab dynasties, with countless markets, the Katoubia Mosque, Jemaa el Fna - a square square in North Africa and numerous other famous ones.
